Know Me Better

I am Orlyn Kate Losaria Engada, 19 years old, born on August 21, 2005, and I live in Brgy. Dabong, Janiuay, Iloilo. I love to write short stories and poems, read Wattpad stories, and watch movies, K-dramas, and anime.

My characteristics are shy, innocent, humble, and kind. I am a quiet person, so I have this attitude that if you won’t approach or talk to me, automatically I won’t even dare to say a word or have a conversation with you.

My dreams are to graduate from this course, save money, help my family, and enjoy myself doing the things I’ve wanted, like traveling around the world to make moments into memories.
